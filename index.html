Isaac Gonzalez

UX / Product Engineer

Hello! I’m Isaac, a recent college graduate from the University of California, Berkeley where I received my Bachelor’s in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science.

I’m an interdisciplinary product engineer situated at the intersection of UX design, human-computer interaction, and artificial intelligence, all of which I’ve primarily worked in with the focus on creating seamless or adaptable systems. Broadly, I’ve studied UI/UX, algorithms, design research (RtD), HCI future interactions, computer graphics, signals, and robotics. Throughout my college career, I’ve grown to learn and enjoy the engineering design process from end-to-end wherever the opportunity presents itself, along with taking on the role as technical project manager or team lead when kickstarting a team to take on a real-world challenge.

I have worked with research and project teams from universities such as UC Berkeley, Cal State Fullerton, Cal State Long Beach, Baylor University, and Vanderbilt University, along with labs including the BAIR (Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research) Lab, the FHL Vive Center for Enhanced Reality, and the Jacobs Design Institute. Organizations I’ve worked with, or have been contracted to, include NASA, Turing, and DeepMind.

Aside from my work, I enjoy running, traveling, working out at the gym, practicing the acoustic guitar, rooting for my favorite sports teams, playing basketball, video games, biking, and bouldering!

I am currently looking for a full-time position within my focus areas of study!
